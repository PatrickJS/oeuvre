THE MAGIC OF OEUVRE
Oeuvre enables art investors to collectively co-invest in art acquisitions sourced, acquired, managed and resold by leading art dealers and art enthusiasts to help art museums finance new commissioned artwork.

The Investment Process Simplified

1

Art dealers source art to acquire

2

Art dealers offer co-investment opportunity in pieces of art

3

Investors co-invest with art dealers and other investors

4

Art dealers acquire, manage and resell the art

5

Upon resale, proceeds proportionally distributed to co-investors
